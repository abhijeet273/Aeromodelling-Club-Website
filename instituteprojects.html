Institute Project

Discover & Learn 1234 - Drones for high altitude & high wind operations

OBJECTIVE :- To design a UAV which can produce high thrust (or) mass flow rate( at ambient conditions) to account for the thinner air at high altitudes and perform necessary operations at high altitudes. The primary purpose of the drones being delivering more payload than any conventional aircraft for the same mass using less energy.
