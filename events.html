Events Calendar

Boeing the leading tech giant of the Aeronautics Industry has been conducting annual National Aeromodelling Competitions in 4 zonals since past 6 years. We as a part of the club’s culture compete in these zonal competitions to Qualify for finals.

Passion remains as a forgotten dream if you don’t tend to it. If you are passionate about Flying, join us and weave your dreams into reality.

The Technical fest of IIT Delhi organised by CAIC. As a part of the Technical clubs in CAIC Community, we organise several events such as Glider Making Competition, RC aircraft Competition and Stall presentation. Over the years the Glider Making Challenge proved to be a crowd gatherer for Tryst.

The most awaited event of the year. Never fails to surprise the judges let alone the audience. The best of the best from all the 4 Boeing zonal competitions go head on in a fast paced competition that keeps viewers on their nerves the whole time. Every year this event witnesses many representatives from Boeing and several professors from various IITs.

Every year, Board of Student Welfare(BSW) & Student Mentorship Program(SMP) jointly organise Tour of stalls, where in various models of Remote Controlled (RC) Aircrafts, Drones are put to display and the Freshmen are given brief introduction to club activities. A short demonstration of working models is also done.

We take pride in flying, so do we take pride in saluting our National Flag. Flag hoisting with a Drone, such an enthralling experience, We assure you goosebumps at this event.

Is it an Exhibition or an Evaluation? Wait, It’s both. After the summer season, all the projects undertaken by the members of Aeromodelling Club are open for an exhibition when they display their work to the students of IIT Delhi. In the meanwhile an Evaluation of their work is also done by the presiding Faculty incharge of the club.

So what you feel you don’t have enough knowledge about basics of Aeromodelling. Say no more. This series of Lectures and Workshops that are a part of recruitment drive aims to provide all the interested students the basic knowledge of Aeromodelling.
